Illona Addae

Illona Addae is a determined and God-fearing young woman with a passion for both programming and acting. I am talent of Fliika Academy and currently pursuing Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science, which has laid the foundation for my journey as a full stack developer. My love for programming runs deep, and I take pride in my skills and proficiency in crafting innovative digital solutions. While I pursue my dreams in the world of technology, I strongly believe in advocating for equal rights to opportunities for both women and men. I firmly believe that every individual, regardless of gender, deserves respect and recognition for their contributions.

My website is a creative space where I aspire to make a positive impact on lives. Through inspirational and motivational quotes, I aim to inspire women, in particular, while also providing valuable insights for men. I believe in the power of words to uplift and drive change, and I'm dedicated to using my skills as a programmer and designer to share these messages across various social media platforms.

As an actor, I embrace the art of storytelling and aim to weave my creativity into every project I undertake. My goal is to become a great person who can influence and empower others. Oceanic Code is not just a website but a platform where I envision fostering a community of like-minded individuals who champion gender equality, empowerment, and personal growth.
